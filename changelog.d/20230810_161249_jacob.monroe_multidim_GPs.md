- Scaling of GPR inputs (`x_scale_fac` argument in `HeteroscedasticGPR`)

- Updated `make_rbf_expr` in `active_utils` (old 1D in `make_rbf_expr_old`)
- Updated `DerivativeKernel`, `HetGaussianDeriv`, `HeteroscedasticGPR` in
  `gpr_models`
